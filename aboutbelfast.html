Our grand city blossomed from very humble beginnings… It started out as a small hamlet with fertile land along the mouth of the Lagan. Soon, marauders and pirates realised our lough was deep and our shores were safe from the north Atlantic storms. The perfect haven for repairing sea ravaged ships. From these early stages the industrious locals developed a boat building trade. And the rest is our history.

So what's Belfast like today?

Well, it's packed with history, culture, exciting events, great food, super shopping… you might even spot a ship or two. You'll also find some of the friendliest people you'll ever meet. Have a look around and learn more about the city we're so proud of.

Beyond Belfast

The variety of our small country is amazing!!

From rolling landscapes and hazy mountains, intriguing towns, and villages to some of the most vibrant, cultural and history rich cities.
